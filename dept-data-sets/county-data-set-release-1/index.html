County Data Set Release 1

Regarding my conversation with Michael Davis, I asked him to the data sets he used for his strategic plan. Once I see who the data stewards are I can add them to the list of departments. Here is what I am looking for in order of importance for the county:

1. Budget Data

2. Public Safety Data

  • Sheriff
  • Index Crime Rate- FBI UCR for City of Durham and Durham County
  • Number of domestic violence orders issued over time
  • Stop and Frisk data
  • EMS
  • Fire, Fire Permit Violations

3. Library Data

  • Circulation
  • Budget
  • Programs
  • Inventory
  • Levels of engagement by inventory type

4. Joint City/County Initiatives

  • Durham Youthwork Internship Program
  • Durham on the Move” / Bull City Play Streets health initiative
  • CPR/AED training: The City, County and Durham Public Schools

5. Joint Strategic Initiatives

  • High school graduation rate
  • Unemployment rate
  • Number of attendees at cultural activities 
  • Number of individuals receiving assistance through the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program. 
  • Number of new, direct jobs created through County economic development incentives

6. Healthy Communities

  • Emergency room admissions
  • Incidence of cancer, heart disease and diabetes in adults
  • Percentage of adults and youth getting recommended physical exercise
  • Number of Durham County residents admitted to State psychiatric hospitals
  • Percentage of obese youth and adults
  • Rate of sudden cardiac arrest victims receiving bystander CPR over time
  • Community Bystander CPR Training numbers and aggregates of availability

7. Environmental Sustainability

  • Acres of open space and farmland protected from development by Durham County
  • Greenhouse gas emissions from the built environment
  • Percentage of Streams Monitored in Durham County with all scores
  • Tons of waste diverted for recycling in the unincorporated areas
  • Total number of violations on the NC Water Quality Data Assessment 303(d) list

8. Election Data: Votes; Campaign Finance; Lobbyists

  • Current Elections: Schedule, Absentee, Early Voting
  • Polling Locations
  • Durham County Officials: Contact Information, Terms, Districts and 
  • Political PAC-Party List
  • Campaign Finance Data
  • Official Elections Results
  • Redistricting Information
  • Precinct Officials
  • Board of Elections: Meetings, Staff

This is not a complete list but it is a good start.
