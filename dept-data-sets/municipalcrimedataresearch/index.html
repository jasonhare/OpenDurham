Municipal Crime Data Research

Questions we asked in our research:

  • What exists -- what data is already out there? (Is it out there?)
  • How does it exist -- which local governments are publishing it, is there outside group involvement, how complete is the data, is it in open format, etc.?
  • Who uses this data -- what constituencies ask for and/or wield it (reporters, NGOs, etc.)?
  • What other data sets does it interact with naturally? -- what data adds context to this dataset
  • What is the ultimate impact of having this information open? -- how have reporters and other watchdogs used this data to tell stories about government and influence?
