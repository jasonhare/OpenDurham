February 10th Meeting Notes

  • Recap of last week's session to catch up Anthony and Kerry.
  • Review Open Data Process document developed by Jason and attached to the wiki
  • Discussion of TODD15. OpenDataSoft will be attending. Microsoft wants to write a case study.
  • Open Data PO has run out.
  • Comms PO for Kim per Les and Beverly
  • Discussions with Josh and Michael on Strategic Initiatives. Use this as a vehicle to get more data sets. Have data sets align to strategic initiatives.
Most of the discussion centered around how to create or whether to create an open data resolution or a policy. Jason suggested a persuasive model to get agencies and business units on board. The City CIO suggested Jason test that method on the Durham Police Chief and Jason agreed to do so. 

Jason brought up the PO being expired. Jason suggested reducing the number of hours until a new PO could be issued. There was also a discussion of the comms PO for a communications person.

After the meeting there was a discussion around the story-board for the CIOs Open Data Data presentation.
